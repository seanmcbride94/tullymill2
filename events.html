Private & Corporate Event Catering

For your most important family occasions and celebrations we offer the best of Fermanagh's hospitality in our restaurant. Our aim is to offer an experience unique to you and create a memory that will stay with you and those who celebrate with you.

From gala balls to sporting events, barbecues to VIP receptions, special occasion parties, or christenings, we have the experience to cater for any and every event. Our dinner menu is available for you or we are happy to create a menu to suit your tastes.

We are also happy to bring the same style food to you, so you can enjoy your family gathering in the comfort of your own home. We take all your catering worries away so that you can enjoy your special occasions.

Or if you would prefer, you can hold your private function in the Largeness Center across from the Tully Mill Restaurant. Here we can offer you a buffet styled menu where you will be sure to have the venue exclusively .
